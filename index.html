Telegram + gestures

Corner-based mouse gestures; you control bot token and data.

How the stealth flow works

  1. Mouse to top-left corner ~2s → start selection.
  2. Click top-left of question, then bottom-right of answers.
  3. Mouse to bottom-right corner ~2s → send and get answer via Telegram.
  4. Top-right corner ~2s to reset.

Setup

Create a bot with @BotFather, add token and chat ID to config.txt. Use OLLAMA (e.g. ollama pull llama3) and Tesseract OCR.

Custom Portal Solutions

Front doors to your internal universe

Tailored portals for teams, partners & customers

We design and build custom web portals that feel native to your workflows: single sign-on, role-based access, dashboards and integrations with your existing systems. From lightweight dashboards to full multi-tenant portals, everything is tuned to your actual processes instead of forcing you into a template.

The architecture is deliberately modular and evolvable: we start with a minimal core and add modules (analytics, training, self-service, integrations) as you grow, avoiding big-bang rewrites. Using a Wardley Mapping–inspired approach, we separate what should remain highly custom from what can be industrialized, so you keep the same portal shell while the capabilities expand over time.

Employee & team portals

Centralize internal tools, documentation, KPIs and forms into a single, secure starting point for your staff.

Partner / client access

Offer self-service access to reports, orders, tickets or training material with fine-grained permissions.

Integrations that stick

Connect to your existing systems (ERP, CRM, LMS, bespoke APIs) so portals stay in sync instead of duplicating data.

Engagement model

Portal projects are scoped and priced individually based on complexity, integrations and user counts. If you are considering a custom portal, we can start with a short discovery call and a small prototype to de-risk the bigger rollout.
